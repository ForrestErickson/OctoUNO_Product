# OctoUNO_Product 
A "marketing" product specification.

## Product specification for OctoUNO_Product  
### Make an elementary curver tracer out of an Arduino UNO. Display on a Processing program
### A free open design for public release


Feature list:
- Mimimal cost external electrical components (<1.00 exclusive of a PCB and connectors and enclosure)
- Can be assembled on solderless breadboard if you need in a hurry.
- Voltage "compliance", the range of test of the DUT is plus and minus about 2.5 volts.
- Display free runs and acumulates curves but can be cleared.
- Display background color can be changed
- Heartbeat LED Winks
